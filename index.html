Now Is the Time

It would be easier to just stay home. It would be cheaper not to do it. They probably won't even notice — and almost certainly won't fully appreciate it. It may well save you a meltdown or a disastrous afternoon. But now is the time.

Later, you won't be able to go to these museums or these concerts or their favorite park or get this many uninterrupted days together. Later, you won't have this kind of decision-making power. Later, they won't be so open to doing things with you. This is their childhood. This is when you have a young family.

Now is the time to take the trip. Now is the time to make the plans. Now is the time to be busy. Now is the time to have the experiences. Now is the time to say yes. Even though it's crazy. Even though you're tired. Even though it might end in a disaster.

You won't remember the downside risk. You won't remember the bad times. You'll remember the stuff you did together. And so will they.

Make Your Notion Agents More Autonomous

A hidden workflow that lets you wake up any Notion Agent, give it tasks, and get a report on what it did — turning passive agents into autonomous workers.

  1. Create a Notion agent from the "Agents" option in the sidebar. Then, open a database for the agent's prompt and task reports.
  2. Click the New dropdown, open Templates, and create a @Today template (e.g., "Daily Summary"). The @Today tag makes duplicates inherit the current date.
  3. Set the properties, write task instructions in the page body, and @mention the agent so the duplicate triggers it. Stop the agent first so it doesn't overwrite the template.
  4. From the template, click New template, then Duplicate, and pick a cadence (e.g., daily at 7 AM). A small blue icon next to the template confirms it's live.

Markets Morning Brew

SEC Proposes Ending Quarterly Reporting

Following President Trump's demand, the SEC formally introduced a proposal for twice-a-year company filings instead of quarterly reports.

Mandatory quarterly reports have been required since 1970 and aim to provide transparency for shareholders. Some business leaders say they're time-consuming and promote short-term thinking. Proponents of the current rule argue that less-frequent reporting would lead to more market volatility. The change could still take years to implement.

Meta Will Use AI to Scan Your Bones to Guess Your Age

Meta announced it will use AI to scan photos and videos for visual cues like height and bone structure to flag users under 13 on Facebook and Instagram.

The system pairs visual analysis with text and behavior signals such as birthday posts or school grade mentions. Flagged accounts are deactivated, and users must complete age verification to avoid permanent deletion. The rollout follows a March ruling in which a New Mexico jury ordered Meta to pay $375 million for misleading users about child safety.

Roomba Creator Unveils Furry AI Robot Companion

Colin Angle, the former iRobot CEO, revealed the Familiar — a life-size, pet-shaped AI robot designed as a cure for loneliness.

The creature doesn't talk but can purr and meow, and is meant to be emotionally intelligent enough to respond to your mood. Angle also sees applications in eldercare and parental support. When asked about growing reliance on technology for emotional needs, Angle said the inability to speak will soften that potentially dangerous connection. The price will be "around the same as pet ownership" — though he didn't specify if that meant a purebred dog or a goldfish.
