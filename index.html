Don’t Let This Hold Them Back From Learning

You made all sorts of sacrifices to get them into this school. It’s not cheap. You love their teachers. You want them to learn. You want them to love learning.

Which is why you shouldn’t hesitate to pull them out of class. For the work trip they can tag along on. For the protest downtown. For a three-day weekend in the mountains. For a visit with their grandparents. For a quiet day at the museum together.

The world is a classroom. There are so many places, so many ways for them to be exposed to things, to see things, to learn things. School matters, but we should never be passing up these other opportunities for the sake of their attendance record.

Pick them up early. Drop them off late. Skip a day. Let their teacher know. Your job is to show your kids the world. Your job is to teach them. Your job is to spend time with them in this brief window when you can. No one regrets taking those chances—but we will regret missing some of them. Do it while you can.

Run Your Own Free Coding Agent on Your Laptop

Learn how to download a coding LLM to your laptop using Ollama and run it for free inside popular coding tools like Claude Code or Codex.

  1. Screenshot your PC hardware specs, drop them into Claude or ChatGPT, and ask which Ollama coder model you can run
  2. Browse coder models, choose the best-suited one, and grab its launch command from Ollama
  3. Open a terminal in your project folder, paste the command, download, and wait. Once done, Ollama drops you into Claude Code, pointed at the local model
  4. Go to Ollama → Settings → Context. Bump the default 4K to 32K or higher. If Claude Code refuses tasks or drops tool calls, try OpenCode the same way

Pro tip: Local AI is not a replacement for paid Claude Code/Codex. Treat it as practice or basic debugging. You can wire it as a cheap subagent under the paid Claude Code.

Trump Signs Executive Order Fast-Tracking Psychedelic Drug Review Morning Brew 1% Better

The order directs the FDA to expedite review of psychedelic compounds including ibogaine and LSD for treating depression, PTSD, and veteran mental health. Joe Rogan stood behind Trump for the signing.

The order also instructs the DEA to begin rescheduling reviews once drugs clear Phase 3 trials, opens a right-to-try pathway for ibogaine access, and commits $50 million in federal funds to match state-level research investments. A Stanford study cited at the signing showed an 80–90% reduction in depression and anxiety symptoms among special operations veterans treated with ibogaine. The VA is now participating in at least five psychedelic drug trials.
